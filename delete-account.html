Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users of Your App Name can delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Account deletion for eligible users can be completed directly from within the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Your App Name mobile application
  2. Log in using your registered username and password
  3. Go to the bottom navigation and select More
  4. Open Account Settings
  5. Select Delete Account
  6. A confirmation modal will appear
  7. Enter your account password to verify ownership
  8. Tap on Delete Account to confirm

Once confirmed, the account is permanently deleted immediately and the user is automatically logged out.

Fraud Prevention

To prevent unauthorized or fraudulent deletion, password verification is mandatory before completing the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

After account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • User account credentials (username & password)
  • Profile information (such as state and city)
  • Application preferences and settings
  • Push notification tokens
  • Any data linked exclusively to the deleted account

Data Retention Policy

  • All personal data is deleted immediately after confirmation
  • System backups (if any) are automatically removed within 30 days
  • No personal data is retained beyond this period

Role-Based Account Deletion

Account deletion rules depend on the role assigned within Your App Name:

  • Public Users:
    Public users create their own accounts and can permanently delete their accounts directly from the app using the Delete Account option.
  • Imaam Accounts:
    Imaam accounts are created and managed by the application administrator. Account deletion for these users is handled by the admin upon request.
  • Admin Accounts:
    Admin accounts are internal system accounts created and managed by the developer and are not intended for public use. These accounts are deleted manually from the database when required.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No payment or banking information
  • No continuous or background location tracking
  • No personal data is sold or shared with third parties
