Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the application ("App") collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information We Collect

To use this App, users are required to create an account. During signup, we collect the following information:

  • Username
  • Password (stored securely in encrypted form)
  • State (selected from dropdown)
  • City (selected from dropdown)

After login, the App may request permission for:

  • Push Notifications, used to send important updates such as Azaan alerts or app-related information.

How We Use the Information

The collected information is used to:

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Display location-based Namaz and Azaan timings
  • Send push notifications related to prayers or app updates
  • Improve app functionality and user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage and Security

User data is stored securely using industry-standard security practices. Reasonable measures are taken to protect data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services such as Firebase for push notifications. These services may collect limited technical data as per their own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be effective once updated within the App.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us using the details provided in the App.
