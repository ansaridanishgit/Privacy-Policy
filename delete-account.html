Account & Data Deletion

This page explains how users of Your App Name can permanently delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

Account deletion can be completed directly from within the app by following these steps:

  1. Open the Your App Name mobile application
  2. Log in using your registered username and password
  3. Go to the bottom navigation and select More
  4. Open Account Settings
  5. Select Delete Account
  6. A confirmation modal will appear
  7. Enter your account password to verify ownership
  8. Tap on Delete Account to confirm

Once confirmed, the account is permanently deleted instantly and the user is automatically logged out.

Fraud Prevention

To prevent unauthorized or fraudulent deletion, password verification is mandatory before completing the deletion request.

Data That Will Be Deleted

After account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • User account credentials (username & password)
  • Profile details (such as state and city)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Push notification tokens
  • Any data linked exclusively to the deleted account

Data Retention Policy

  • Account and personal data are deleted immediately after confirmation
  • System backups (if any) are automatically cleared within 30 days
  • No personal data is retained after this period

Data We Do Not Collect

  • No payment or banking information
  • No continuous location tracking
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties
